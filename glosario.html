Technical Glossary

18 términos · Color, Diseño & Tecnología

Vocabulary

Términos aprendidos en el evento

Mínimo 15 términos técnicos en inglés relacionados con color, diseño, UX/UI y tecnología — con traducción al español y definición completa.

English Term Español Definition Category
Color Theory Teoría del Color Study of how colors generate visual perception, emotion and meaning in design, art and communication contexts. Includes concepts like hue, saturation, value and harmony. Color
Pantone Pantone Internationally standardized color identification system used by designers, printers and manufacturers to ensure color consistency across different materials and media. Color
Semiology Semiología Study of signs and symbols and their role in cultural and visual communication. In design, it explains how colors function as signs that carry specific cultural meanings. Color
Color Palette Paleta de Colores A curated set of colors selected and combined to achieve visual harmony and communicate a specific brand identity, emotion or aesthetic in a design project. Color
Color Harmony Armonía del Color Pleasing arrangement of colors based on their positions on the color wheel. Types include complementary, analogous, triadic and square harmonies, used widely in UI design. Color
Hue Matiz The pure attribute of a color identified by its position on the visible spectrum (red, yellow, blue, etc.). It is the property that distinguishes one color family from another. Color
Saturation Saturación The intensity or purity of a color. A fully saturated color appears vivid and bright, while a desaturated color appears muted, grayish or washed out. Color
UX Design Diseño de Experiencia de Usuario Process of designing products and digital environments that provide meaningful, accessible and relevant experiences. Focuses on user needs, behavior and interaction flows. Design
UI Design Diseño de Interfaz de Usuario Discipline focused on the visual and interactive elements of digital products: buttons, typography, spacing, iconography, color and layout that the user directly sees and touches. Design
Visual Hierarchy Jerarquía Visual Arrangement and presentation of design elements that guides the viewer's eye through content in order of importance, using size, color, contrast and position as tools. Design
Brand Identity Identidad de Marca The complete set of visual and conceptual elements that differentiate a brand: logo, color palette, typography, tone of voice and imagery that convey its personality and values. Design
Typography Tipografía Art and technique of arranging typefaces to make written content legible, readable and visually appealing. Includes choices of font family, weight, size, spacing and alignment. Design
Accessibility Accesibilidad Design principle ensuring that digital products are usable by people of all abilities and disabilities. Includes sufficient color contrast, keyboard navigation and screen reader support. Design
Responsive Design Diseño Responsive Web design approach that creates interfaces that adapt fluidly to different screen sizes, from mobile phones to large desktop monitors, ensuring a consistent user experience. Tech
Grid System Sistema de Rejilla Structural framework used in design and web development to organize content in consistent, aligned columns and rows. Bootstrap's 12-column grid is a widely used implementation. Tech
Circular Economy Economía Circular Economic model focused on eliminating waste through continuous use of resources — reusing, repairing, refurbishing and recycling products and materials to extend their lifecycle. Tech
Sustainable Design Diseño Sostenible Design approach that minimizes environmental impact while maximizing usability, longevity and ethical responsibility throughout a product's lifecycle, from creation to disposal. Tech
GitHub Pages GitHub Pages Free static site hosting service provided by GitHub that allows developers to publish web projects directly from a repository. Used here to deploy this academic portfolio publicly. Tech
